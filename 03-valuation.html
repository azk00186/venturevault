Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI valuation? +
Our AI valuation is based on real UK comparable sales data and sector-specific multiples. It provides a strong starting point and is typically within 15–25% of final sale price. For a more precise valuation, we recommend combining this with a free expert consultation.
Is it really free? +
Yes, completely free — no credit card, no sign-up, no hidden fees. VentureVault only earns a fee when a business sale completes. Everything before that is provided at no cost.
What factors affect my business value? +
The key factors are: annual turnover and net profit, trading history and growth trend, sector (some sectors command higher multiples), location and footfall, tenure type (freehold worth more than short leasehold), staff dependency, and current condition of premises.
Can I use this valuation to list my business? +
Absolutely. Once you have your AI valuation, you can click "List at This Price" to go straight to our seller listing form. Your valuation will be pre-filled. You can adjust the asking price at any time.
What is an EBITDA multiple? +
EBITDA stands for Earnings Before Interest, Tax, Depreciation and Amortisation — essentially your profit before accounting adjustments. A business sold at "3x EBITDA" means the sale price was three times the annual profit. Different sectors have different typical multiples: e.g. pubs often sell at 2–3x, care homes at 5–8x.
How do I increase my business value before selling? +
Key ways to boost value: increase profitability (even small improvements compound into significant value increases), reduce owner dependency by having good staff, secure a longer lease, update accounts and financial records, improve hygiene ratings and Google reviews, and fix any deferred maintenance.
