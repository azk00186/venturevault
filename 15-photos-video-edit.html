📖 Full Business DescriptionAI-written from seller interview · Seller approved

This is a rare opportunity to acquire a thriving, fully refurbished sports bar and restaurant in a prime Maidstone high street location. Established in 2010 and comprehensively refurbished in 2024 at a cost of £60,000, the business is presented in exceptional condition and is ready for immediate trade under new ownership.

The premises extend to approximately 2,250 square feet comprising a main bar with 80 covers, fully equipped commercial kitchen, private function room for 30 guests, a new beer garden with all-weather cover, and a temperature-controlled cellar. Held on a secure 18-year leasehold at a competitive rent, with rent review not due for 3 years.

Trading 7 days a week, the business generates a consistent weekly turnover of approximately £18,000, with peak revenue Thursday–Saturday driven by live sports events. Full premises licence including 24-hour alcohol, entertainment and late-night refreshment. 5-Star FSA hygiene rating.

The business employs 12 staff — 2 full-time managers, 4 full-time bar/kitchen, 6 part-time — all expected to remain under TUPE. Owner works 3 days per week supervisory. Up to 8 weeks handover offered. All equipment included: 18 screens, commercial kitchen, EPOS system, DJ booth, all furniture.

The owner is selling after 14 years due to retirement and is highly motivated to find the right buyer to continue the business's excellent reputation.
